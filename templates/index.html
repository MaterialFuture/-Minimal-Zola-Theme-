Welcome,

Who Am I?

MaterialFuture / ˈmətɪrɪəl fjtʊər/ - A programmer, UX Researcher, Writer, SysAdmin, and Web Developer. I create software that helps people's lives easier without comprimizing on their freedoms. I also write articles regarding my life as well as what I've learned in my professional life.

My Work

I mainly work on offline-compatible application that require's very little from the user to get working. Examples of software projects I'm working on are listed on the projects page.

For non-software related work, I research User Experience and write papers in regards to that. You can read my papers here or you can search the tags of my posts and find any relevant tags regarding my writings.

My Goals

My goals are constantly changing as I want to constantly improve so there's no end-goal just a constantly and consistant pursuit towards bettering myself and others. I focus primarily on offline application and technologies and hope by end of 2020 to have the services that I use to be either decentralized and in my control or be offline only. The reason for this is too long to write here, so I'll link to a blog post when the time comes.

This Site

This site was initially a place for me to put my blog posts and links to things, but that has since changed. This site is also a personal wiki for personal and public use that defines different aspects of what I do. You can consider this part Blog/Wiki/Resource for all of what I do.

I also try and provide links to software alternative as well as useful links for people to link to and share.

This site is offline capable, running nicely on Lynx, and contains an RSS feed for posts.
